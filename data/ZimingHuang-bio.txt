Ziming Huang is a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer specializing in bioinformatics and drug discovery, currently based in Munich. He is a PhD candidate in Medical Research at LMU Klinikum and Helmholtz München (expected 2026), where his research focuses on dissecting cellular heterogeneity in cardiovascular immunology using single-cell genomics and spatial transcriptomics. He received an MSc in AI-assisted Drug Design from the Shanghai Institute of Materia Medica in 2022 and a BSc in Bioinformatics from Huazhong Agricultural University.
